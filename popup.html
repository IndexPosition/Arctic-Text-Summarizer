About Arctic

Arctic is a powerful Chrome extension that makes summarizing text easier than ever. Whether you're dealing with lengthy articles, detailed research papers, or dense documents, Arctic helps you distill the key points quickly and efficiently.

How It Works

Using Arctic is straightforward. Press Ctrl + Q (or Cmd + Q on Mac) to open the summarization tool. Simply paste your text into the popup, and Arctic will provide a concise summary in seconds.
